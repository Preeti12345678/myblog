My favorite character in deltora quest!

Jasmine



Jasmine is a fourteen year old girl and when she was younger she was a wild girl growing up in the Forests of Silence, and because of this she is capable of understanding and speaking to animals and trees. Her closest friends are the raven Kree and a Siskis named Filli. Jasmine is the daughter of Jarred and Anna.Lief and Barda met Jasmine in the beginning of their quest to restore the seven gems to the Belt of Deltora and joined them.
